# relation to other teams

For every product we arrange the digital home based on how it will be used

• Convention
• JW Broadcasting
• Midweek meeting
• Schools
• Advertised to the public

Pubman for the translation and production materials released to branches

MediaCenter for the video master materials

Convention: set up the metadata and media playlist so videos can be played in order at convention venue with each talk. Includes songs, preludes, talk titles, videos. Digital publisher uses this Word doc to create the digital media playlist.

For midweek meeting we prepare a list of videos so that branches can confirm they have translated the video. We provide up-to-date translation materials for all videos on Midweek Workbook for languages that need to prepare older videos. The digital publisher uses our video to confirm the file names for video links in the workbook.

If a video will be advertised to the public, we will work with digital publishers to prepare a video hosting document.

Work with Composition team to prepare the lead sheet for Original songs.

Work with Audio Description team to enable metadata in MediaCenter and publish their translation material for audio descriptions for videos for MWM, convention, JWB

Work with Sign Language Team to enable and publish their alternate master and materials for music videos, convention preludes, and circuit assembly preludes

Provide Early mailing/Final mailing dates so branches can plan their work. Also provides MEPS information on when to release these products.
